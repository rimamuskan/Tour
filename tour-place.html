Kolkata, India Tour

Day 1

Kolkata - City of Joy

Arrive in Kolkata, the cultural capital of India, and immerse yourself in the city's rich history and vibrant culture. Explore iconic landmarks like the Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge, and indulge in the local street food at Park Street. Experience the cultural charm of Kolkata with a visit to Dakshineswar Kali Temple and the Mother House, the headquarters of Missionaries of Charity founded by Mother Teresa.

Day 2

Kashmir - Heaven on Earth

Conclude the journey in Kashmir, often referred to as "Heaven on Earth." Explore the Dal Lake with a shikara ride and visit the iconic Mughal Gardens. Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage at Shankaracharya Temple and Hazratbal Shrine. Enjoy the breathtaking views of the Himalayas, and if possible, experience the unique houseboat accommodation on Dal Lake before departing.

Day 3

Udaipur - City of Lakes

Travel to Udaipur, known as the "City of Lakes." Explore the picturesque City Palace, overlooking Lake Pichola, and visit the serene Jag Mandir. Take a boat ride on Lake Pichola, marveling at the stunning views of the palaces and temples along the shores. Wander through the vibrant markets and enjoy the cultural performances in the evening.

Day 4

Mumbai - City of Dreams

Head to Mumbai, the bustling metropolis known as the "City of Dreams." Visit iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India and Marine Drive. Explore the historic Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus and the vibrant Colaba Causeway for shopping. Dive into the cultural scene at the Prince of Wales Museum and enjoy the evening at Juhu Beach or the lively streets of Bandra

Day 5

Jaipur - The Pink City and Jal Mahal

Travel to Jaipur, known as the Pink City, and immerse yourself in its regal history and vibrant culture. Explore the majestic Amber Fort, perched atop a hill, and marvel at its intricate architecture. Visit the City Palace, a stunning blend of Rajasthani and Mughal styles, and wander through the historic Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds). In the afternoon, head to the serene Jal Mahal (Water Palace), a unique architectural gem in the middle of Man Sagar Lake. Enjoy the picturesque surroundings and capture the beauty of the palace against the backdrop of the lake. Conclude your day with a visit to the bustling local markets, where you can shop for traditional handicrafts, textiles, and jewelry. Jaipur's royal charm and architectural splendors promise a memorable end to your journey through the cultural tapestry of India.

Day 6

Agra - Majestic Taj Mahal

Embark on a scenic journey to Agra, home to one of the Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal. Spend the day exploring this architectural masterpiece, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife. Marvel at the intricate marble inlays, stroll through the enchanting gardens, and absorb the historical significance of this iconic monument. Visit Agra Fort, another UNESCO World Heritage Site, showcasing a blend of Persian and Mughal architecture. Conclude your day with a mesmerizing view of the Taj Mahal at sunset before departing for your onward journey or next adventure.
